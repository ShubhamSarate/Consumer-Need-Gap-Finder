💡 Simple Dashboard Guide

1. What does this dashboard do?

It reads 6,000 real customer reviews from 14 Indian D2C competitor brands (like Minimalist, Oziva, WOW) and finds what customers hate or feel missing. You can use these gaps to launch better products.

2. What is an "Opportunity Priority Score"?

A score from 1 to 100 indicating how big the opportunity is. A higher score means many customers are angry about this issue and no competitor has solved it yet.

3. How do I use the 3 Tabs?

  • Top Customer Problems: View all 15 customer complaints ranked from highest opportunity to lowest. Click any problem to see reviews & brand impact.
  • Browse by Category: Filter problems by Skin Care, Hair Care, Supplements, Men's Grooming, or Body Care.
  • New Product Solutions: View step-by-step product specifications, formulas, and launch timelines to solve these gaps.
